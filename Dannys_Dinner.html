Dannys Dinner

Overview

The 'Danny's Dinner' SQL project, part of the #8WeekSQLChallenge, is a comprehensive exploration into the intricacies of SQL to derive valuable insights from a restaurant's database. This initiative aims to dissect data, unraveling revenue patterns, and pinpointing menu favorites. The focus is on strategic data utilization to enhance the overall success of Danny's Dinner, navigating through a complex data landscape.

Project Introduction

In the beginning of 2021, Danny Ma ventured into a risky yet passion-driven venture, opening 'Danny’s Diner' specializing in his top three favorite Japanese foods: sushi, curry, and ramen. The restaurant, 'Danny’s Diner,' seeks assistance to translate their basic operational data into valuable insights for better business management. The primary goal is to understand customer visiting patterns, spending habits, and menu preferences to enhance the overall dining experience and potentially expand the existing customer loyalty program.

Process Steps

  • Data Population
  • Data Cleaning
  • In-depth Exploration
  • Insight Generation

Strategic Recommendations

  • Implement targeted promotions or loyalty incentives for Ramen to capitalize on its popularity and encourage repeat purchases.
  • Fine-tune the loyalty program to encourage early enrollment, possibly through initial benefits or exclusive offers, considering the sequential adoption observed in Customer A and B.
  • Expand the loyalty program rewards to accommodate diverse preferences, considering the shift from Curry to Sushi observed in Customer A and B post-enrollment.
  • Develop strategies to enhance engagement with customers like Customer A, who has shown consistent patronage, contributing significantly to overall sales.
  • Leverage insights from spending variations before loyalty program enrollment to tailor promotions or personalized offers based on individual customer spending behaviors.
  • Understand the motivations or preferences of customers like Customer C, who have not opted for the loyalty program, and tailor targeted communication or promotions to enhance their engagement.
