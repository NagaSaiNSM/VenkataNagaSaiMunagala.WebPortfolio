Pizza Runner

Overview

Embarking on the Pizza Runner Challenge, I conducted a meticulous data cleaning and analysis journey to optimize operational efficiency and drive business growth. In collaboration with Danny Ma's visionary concept, this project delved into customer behaviors, operational intricacies, and financial dynamics. The result is a set of actionable recommendations poised to elevate Pizza Runner's success through strategic data utilization.

Project Introduction

In the realm of data science, the Pizza Runner Challenge aimed at optimizing Danny Ma's visionary venture, Pizza Runner. Introducing "Runners" to address existing supply gaps, Danny's strategic move played a pivotal role in operational refinement. My role as a Data Analyst encompassed holistic analysis, including runner and customer experiences, ingredient optimization, pricing strategies, and ratings. Anchored by robust data cleansing, this project emphasized data's pivotal role in steering Pizza Runner towards operational excellence. The entity relationship diagram served as a testament to informed decision-making and strategic elevation.

Process Steps

  • Data Population
  • Data Cleaning
  • In-depth Exploration
  • Insight Generation

Strategic Recommendations

  • Leverage customer preference insights to design targeted promotions and loyalty programs, enhancing sustained engagement.
  • Streamline operational processes based on the positive correlation between order count and prep time, ensuring efficiency in preparation and timely deliveries.
  • Address varying delivery times through optimization strategies to provide consistent and prompt delivery experiences.
  • Introduce strategic pricing adjustments, such as a $1 fee for extra toppings, to maximize earnings and optimize runner payments based on distance for enhanced net revenue.
  • Refine runner recruitment strategies, capitalizing on the surge in sign-ups during the first week to ensure an efficient and adequate runner fleet.
